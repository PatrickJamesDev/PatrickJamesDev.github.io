Zumo robots competition

This project was assembled in the beginning of the first year of my studies.

The competition consisted of three parts: Sumo fight, maze, pathfinding race.

Technologies:
  • - C
  • - Zumo robots
  • - MQTT
  • - Cloud/Servers
Sumo fight


In the sumo fight competition the robot was supposed to fight opponent and drag it away from the fighting area. My robot took the 3rd place in the tournament.

Path-finding


In the path-finding competition the robot was supposed to follow a path from point A to point B without sliding off the path and not stopping on the false points. During te process robot was sending MQTT messages to the server with info concerning the time it took to complete the path and other info.
