Currency predictions

This project was completed on my own in the time period of y first year in the university.

This was my very first experience of practical work with Data Science and Machine Learning fields. The idea of this smart model is to analyze the Russian currency price in dollars and predict the estimated future price shifts. The data was taken from the Central Bank of Russia data center.

The model uses multiple algorithms like Linear regression, neighbor analysis and other. In the end the model had relatively good predictions of prices and pattern changes.

Technologies:
  • - Python
  • - Anaconda/Jupiter Notebook
  • - Machine Learning
  • - Advanced Mathematics
